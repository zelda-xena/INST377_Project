About Our Project!

Our project was to create a website that uses a public APIs. For this project, the problem that we are trying to solve is for those who is on their night out and they don't know what to order for their drinks. Our website is a drink generator where you can customize some of the elements to your liking or use a surprise generator one. Explore our website and have fun!
