Instructions

Here's how to generate your special drink:

  1. You can choose up to 3 ingredients for your drink!
  2. To generate your drink, you will only need to choose one ingredient. If you would like to, you can choose one or two more ingredients.
  3. Once you have chosen your ingredient(s), click the Generate! button and your drink will be generated.
