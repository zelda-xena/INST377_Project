Instructions

Here's how to generate your special drink!

  1. Choose a flavour that you want or like from the flavour drop down
  2. Choose an ingredient that you want or like from the ingredient drop down
  3. Choose an alcohol that you want as the base for your drink from the alcohol drop down
FYI:
You do not need to choose from all three options! You can choose either one or two options as well, just have fun!
