How to Use Our Site!

There are two options you can choose from in the Home Page:

  1. Ingredient Selector - By using this, you will be taken to a form where you can choose between 1, 2, or 3 ingredients. Once you have chosen your ingredients, click the Generate button, and you will be given a drink with one of the ingredients that you have chosen. The name, image, instructions, and ingredients of the drink will be shown.
  2. Surprise Generator - By using this, you can get a random drink. Once you have clicked the Surprise Me! button, you will be taken to a page where you have to click another button to get a random drink. The name, image, instructions, and ingredients of the drink will be shown.

You can also access our About Page, which gives a brief explanation of why we created the project and how it will help.
